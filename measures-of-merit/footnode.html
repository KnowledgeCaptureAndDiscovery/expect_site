...plans.
In accordance with CHECKMATE usage, the term objectives is used in this document meaning any air campaign objective, i.e. an objective in either one of the four basic levels of Air Campaign Plans described in ACPT: CINC objectives, air objectives, air tasks and air activities. An air objective is thus only one type of objective in air campaign plans.
