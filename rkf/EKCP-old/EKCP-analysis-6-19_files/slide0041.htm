Acquiring BW Process Models
•Three stages :
•I) user specifies the steps in the process model
–What steps are substeps of others, alternative substeps, ordering
•e.g.: Producing dry Anthrax has 3 ordered substeps: ferment, dry and mill
–Can use PSR’s CIM tool, Cmaps, commercial process tools, etc.
•II) user specifies for each step: personnel, material, equipment, duration
•e.g., materials include wet rice
•III) user models specific steps in detail
–Draws from the library components relevant to speficic steps
•e.g., what does fermentation do to Anthrax spores
–User links the materials & equipment specified in stage II to specific roles in the components
•e.g., wet rice is mapped to “growth medium” in the fermentation component
