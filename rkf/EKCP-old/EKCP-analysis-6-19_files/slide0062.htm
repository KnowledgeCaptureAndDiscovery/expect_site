Representing Evaluation Criteria
•Criteria are ultimately ground on detailed knowledge about the BW process models
–Hardiness-in-environment: derived from spore size, whether it is on liquid slurry, env. conditions such as temperature, etc.
–Easiness-to-dispense: derived from spray method used, spore size at time of dispersal, whether powder or liquid, etc.
–Production-feasibility: derived from what equipment is needed & its availability, etc.
