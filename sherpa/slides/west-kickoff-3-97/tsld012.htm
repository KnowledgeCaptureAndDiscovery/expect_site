Conclusion

  • Our approach to knowledge acquisition:

    • Build models of how knowledge is used in applications
    • Use those models to guide users in knowledge acquisition

  • Three main thrusts in our work for HPKB:

    • EXCALIBUR: enhanced framework for KBS development and maintenance
    • LIBRA: support the initial phases of KBS development with tools for problem-solving method libraries
    • SHERPA: an Acquisition Mediator for systems developed outside our framework
