Conclusions
• Domain experts were able to use our KA tools to enter
new COA critiques and adapt existing ones
• Our experiments show that
• our KA techniques (interdependency models, PSM-based KA
scripts, English-based editor) broaden the range of knowledge
that domain experts can enter
– problem-solving knowledge (including procedural constructs)
• our KA techniques help users enter knowledge faster
• A subject with practically no training was able to add
new COA critiques
• KA tool uses background knowledge, NL-based interface, and
acquisition scripts
• We are developing a good methodology to evaluate KA
tools
