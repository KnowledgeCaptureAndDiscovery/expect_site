EXPECT’s Support for KA:
Key Technologies
1) How do I know I am adding the right thing?
• System derives and uses model of knowledge interdependencies
to understand how different pieces of knowledge are related
– Guides users to add the facts needed for problem solving
2) I don’t know the computer language…
• An English-based editor
– Users modify the English paraphrase of the internal, more formal
representation
3) KA takes many steps; users will be lost…
• Script-Based Knowledge Acquisition
– Capture typical sequences of changes that users make
4) Where do I start?
• Capture general principles, core theories (e.g. plan evaluation)
– Can be adapted by user with specific domain knowledge
