EXPECT’s Approach to COA Critiquing:
A Reusable Method for Plan Evaluation
nApproach: Design and reuse of a PSM that captures general principles, in terms of
äPlan ontology: represents manually or automatically created plans, tasks, goals, commitments, causal links, etc.
äCritiquing and evaluation ontology (output ontology): “a critique is the result of analyzing a feature of the plan and determining whether it deviates from a given norm”
äEvaluation PSMs, e.g., “a goal statement that is missing any of its subcomponents is incomplete”
nCurrent scope of PSM:
äAnalysis of the plan structure (e.g., “check missing task decomposition links”)
äAnalysis of resource use (e.g., check that non-shareable resources are not used by different tasks that overlap in time)
