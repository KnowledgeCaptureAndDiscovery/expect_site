EXPECT’s Approach to COA Critiquing:
A Reusable Method for Plan Evaluation
• Approach: Design and reuse of a PSM that captures
general principles, in terms of
• Plan ontology: represents manually or automatically created
plans, tasks, goals, commitments, causal links, etc.
• Critiquing and evaluation ontology (output ontology): “a
critique is the result of analyzing a feature of the plan and
determining whether it deviates from a given norm”
• Evaluation PSMs, e.g., “a goal statement that is missing any
of its subcomponents is incomplete”
• Current scope of PSM:
• Analysis of the plan structure (e.g., “check missing task
decomposition links”)
• Analysis of resource use (e.g., check that non-shareable
resources are not used by different tasks that overlap in time)
