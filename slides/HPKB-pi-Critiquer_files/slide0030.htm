Summary
The Expect critiquer both produced high-quality results
and demonstrated several key HPKB technologies:
• PSM-based critiquer design using middle-level
ontologies and generic problem-solving knowledge.
• Use of a broad spectrum of HPKB ontologies
designed by a number of participants.
• Generation of natural-language explanations without
hand-coded templates.
• Integration of a critiquer with Expect’s knowledge
maintenance and knowledge acquisition capabilities
(see our KA CCE presentation tomorrow).
