Natural-language explanation generation
• EXPECT’s representation language was developed
with explanation generation in mind
• Architecture is based on EES [Swartout and Moore 93;
Swartout et al. 91]
• Thesis: internal representation must reflect how
people reason and explain
• makes it possible to generate natural language explanations
without hand-coded templates
• Natural language descriptions of methods are
automatically combined into a set of linked hypertext
pages to create a critique explanation.
• A prototype used for the evaluation achieved a high
score for justification soundness.
