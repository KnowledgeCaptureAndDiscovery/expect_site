Kinds of knowledge that users need to add

  • Factual information

    • Situation-specific: the amcits are currently located at the embassy
    • Persistent: The embassy is located at these lat-long coordinates

  • New features and classes

    • Add new features: contracts have an upper limit on hotel rates
    • Add new classes: security clearances, new equipment

  • New plan constraints and preferences

    • Preferences and default values: By default, rent from Hertz
    • Plan constraints (e.g. ROEs):
      all activity must be completed during hours of darkness.

  • New planning operators and templates

    • to fly overseas, modify the “fly” template to fill in passport and visa information
