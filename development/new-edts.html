New Notation for EXPECT Data Types (EDTs)

Basic EDTs

 Current notation  New notation
 (INST LA) = LA  (the-instance LA) = LA
 (DESC allied-airport) = allied-airport  (the-concept allied-airport)
 (spec-of factorial)  (subtype-of factorial)
 (inst-of location)  location
 (24 32 5)  (the-set 24 32 5)
= (the-set (the-instance 24)...)
= (the-set 32 24 5)
   (the-list 24 32 5)
 (or 2 3 4)  (one-of 2 3 4)
 (set-of (inst-of number))  (set-of number)
   (list-of number)
(set-of (spec-of constraint))  (set-of (subtype-of constraint))
   (list-of (subtype-of constraint))
