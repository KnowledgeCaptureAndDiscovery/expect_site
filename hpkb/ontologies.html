HPKB Battlespace Ontologies

These ontologies have been developed or are under development at ISI for the HPKB year 2 battlespace problem.

PLANET

This is an ontology for plans, available in Loom , CycL and KIF formats. The Cyc version was developed in collaboration with Cycorp and Teknowledge: here is a document explaining the alignment process. At the moment, the Loom and KIF versions are the more complete. Here is a page describing the ontology, with a pointer to a more detailed project report.

Critiquer output ontology

This ontology of critiques, evaluations and estimates is for use to couch the output of the separate critiquers to the integration module. Here is the ontology in Loom and KIF. Here is a page describing the ontology.

Planning resource and constraints ontology

An ontology of resources and constraints used for evaluating plans. Here is the ontology in Loom and KIF. Here is a page describing the ontology.
